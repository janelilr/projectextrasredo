Filipino Food

Filipino food is filling and delicious! It is a staple at many Filipino gatherings from celebrations to memorials. A big dish of sinigang could feed a family for days and eating halo halo would be refreshing on a hot day in the Philippines. But what makes Filipino food, Filipino food? I found a quote from Andy Kryza from The Thrillest describe it as “a melting pot cuisine that takes elements of Chinese, Malaysian, and mixes in huge amount of Spanish influence.” For me, it’s food that I grew up eating.

The following pages has an assortment of different food items. This is not a complete list of all the food that Filipino cuisine has to offer, but it’s a good start on getting acquainted with foods you might encounter in the Philippines.
